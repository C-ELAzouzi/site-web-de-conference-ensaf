About EmTECH'21

From March 1 to 2, 2021, the first edition of the International Conference on Emerging Computing and Networking Technologies will be held at the National School of Applied Sciences in Fez, Sidi Mohammed Ben Abdellah University. This conference will constitute an opportunity and a space for sharing, contribution and discussion between researchers, academics, scientists and industrial professionals, around innovative ideas in the fields of emerging technologies and their applications. The richness of the themes of the EmTECH'21 conference makes this first edition an opportunity for exchange between researchers, who are invited to submit their research work in relation to the fields of new technologies and their applications ( AI, Networking, E-learning,…). All accepted papers will be sent for publication in indexed journals and the conference proceedings will be published in an indexed database.
